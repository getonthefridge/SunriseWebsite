Meet Our Staff
Dr. George Soliman, MD

Dr. George Soliman is a board-certified physician in Anesthesiology and Pain Management dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care for individuals suffering from injury-related pain. With a strong commitment to clinical excellence and compassionate treatment, Dr. Soliman focuses on helping patients understand their diagnoses, explore appropriate treatment options, and recover through the least aggressive and most effective methods possible.

Dr. Soliman completed his residency in Anesthesiology at Mayo Clinic in Jacksonville, Florida, where he developed a strong foundation in perioperative medicine and advanced anesthetic techniques. He then pursued fellowship training in Interventional Pain Management at Emory University in Atlanta, further refining his expertise in minimally invasive spine and pain procedures.

He believes that education is a cornerstone of healing. By taking the time to thoroughly explain diagnoses and outline evidence-based treatment pathways, Dr. Soliman empowers his patients to make informed decisions about their care. His approach emphasizes thoughtful evaluation, minimally invasive interventions when appropriate, and a collaborative plan designed to restore function and improve quality of life.

Born in Columbus, Ohio, Dr. Soliman now resides in Florida with his wife and three children. Outside of medicine, he enjoys spending time with his family boating, fishing, and golfing. He is also the proud owner of a golden retriever named Beau.

Dr. Soliman remains committed to delivering ethical, patient-focused care with integrity, precision, and compassion.

Brooke Margol, APRN, FNP-BC

Brooke is a Nurse Practitioner dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-centered care in the field of pain management. A North Carolina native, she has proudly called Jacksonville home for more than 30 years and has deep roots in the local community.

She earned both her Bachelor of Science in Nursing and her Master of Science in Nursing with a specialization in Family Medicine at the University of North Florida, building a strong clinical foundation focused on comprehensive, whole-person care. With over 13 years of nursing experience at the Mayo Clinic, she brings a high level of clinical expertise, professionalism, and evidence-based practice to every patient encounter.

Passionate about pain management, Brooke is committed to helping patients improve their quality of life through individualized treatment plans, attentive listening, and a collaborative approach to care.

Outside of work, she enjoys spending time with her husband and two children, traveling, and relaxing at the beach.
